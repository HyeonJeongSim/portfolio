Reset Your Password.

If you forgot your Apple Account password,
Here's how to reset your Apple Account password and regain access to your account.
The easiest way to reset your password is with your iPhone or other trusted Apple device — one that's already signed in to your Apple Account, so that Apple knows that it's yours. You also need a passcode (or password on Mac) set up on that device.
Resetting your password on the web when you don't have a trusted device is another way to regain access to your account.

FAQ

We've organized our FAQs into categories for easy navigation, ensuring you can quickly locate the information you need. Whether you're setting up a new device, troubleshooting an issue, or exploring our services, you'll find clear and concise answers here.

How do I set up my new Apple device?
Setting up your new Apple device is quick and easy. Simply power it on, follow the on-screen instructions, and sign in with your Apple ID. For detailed steps or troubleshooting tips, visit our setup guide or contact our support team.
How can I check my device's warranty or AppleCare coverage?
You can check your warranty or AppleCare coverage by visiting the Check Coverage page and entering your device's serial number. This will provide details about your service and support options.
What are my repair options for a damaged Apple device?
Apple offers a variety of repair options, including in-store service, mail-in repair, and authorized service providers. Visit our Repair and Service page to schedule a repair or explore your options.
How do I back up my data?
Backing up your data ensures that your files, photos, and settings are safe. You can use iCloud to back up automatically or connect your device to a computer and use Finder (Mac) or iTunes (Windows). For a step-by-step guide, visit our Backup Support page.
How do I update my Apple device to the latest software version?
To keep your device running smoothly with the latest features and security updates, simply go to Settings > General > Software Update. If an update is available, follow the prompts to download and install it.
How can I contact Apple Support for help?
You can reach Apple Support by visiting the Apple Support page, where you can chat with an expert, schedule a call, or visit an Apple Store for in-person assistance.
Can I return or exchange my Apple product?
Yes, Apple offers a return or exchange policy for most products within 14 days of purchase. To initiate a return or exchange, visit our Returns & Refunds page for detailed instructions on how to proceed.
How do I manage my iCloud storage?
You can manage your iCloud storage by going to Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage. From there, you can upgrade your storage plan or delete old backups and files to free up space.
How can I activate or deactivate Find My iPhone?
To activate Find My iPhone, go to Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Find My iPhone, and toggle it on. If you need to deactivate it, simply follow the same steps and toggle it off. This feature helps you locate your device in case it's lost or stolen.
